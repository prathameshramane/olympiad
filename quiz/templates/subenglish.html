English

The International School English Olympiad (ISEO) is the english olympiad exam for the year 2020. Classes 1-10 can take part in this exam comprising mocks and one final paper. The Mock Tests can be attempted three times before giving the final exam. ISEO provides a stronger and more dignified grasp over english. The exam pattern is of MCQ (Multiple Choice Questions) type. Students of class 1 to 4 will get minutes to complete the exam and students of class 5 to 10 will get a total of minutes. A comprehensive report divided into various categories ( for example- grammar, comprehension,reading, etc) will be provided to each student on evaluation of results. For any guidance, book recommendation or exam tips please visit the exam preparation of this website.

The exam will be divided into sections as follows:

Class 1-10:   Word and Structure Knowledge
Reading
Spoken and Written Expression
