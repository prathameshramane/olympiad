What's so special about International Olympiads?

With so many Olympiad exams around, you should read the following before you offer these exams to your students

Indeed a unique Olympiad Exam!

  1. Unicus Olympiads is a brainchild of alumni from IIT Delhi, Stanford University, University of Chicago and Oxford University, along with a few other eminent professionals who have worked with the likes of Amazon, Intel, HSBC, Fidelity, GE and A.T. Kearney.

  2. We are India's first Summer Olympiad focusing on student's conceptual understanding of previous classes.

  3. These assessments take place during Summers (May/Jul 2020) when the school curriculum is complete.

  4. We conduct both, online and pen/paper mode of examination. The online examination is completely proctored through webcams. There's a mock test for you to get the exposure of giving an online exam.

  5. There would be 3 Practice Tests in the form of downloadable pdf to understand the format of exam.

Fee Structure and Account Details

For classes 2 to 10, the total fee is Rs. 225 (Rs 150 - Registration fee and Rs 75 - cost of 3 Practice Tests) for every subject per student ($10 for International students). While for class 1, the total fee is Rs. 175 (Rs 100 - Registration fee and Rs 75 - cost of 3 Practice Tests) for every subject (English, Mathematics and Science) per student ($8 for International students). There should be minimum 50 students from a school to participate in Unicus Olympiads.
