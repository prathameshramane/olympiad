Awards And Recognition

Every year numerous students from all across the globe participate in Olympiads to earn their spots and scholarships to attain their ambitions by following their dreams. So, to encourage young minds Olympiads every year invest enormous amounts of money on scholarships, certificates and prizes which works as essential credentials. Previously it was estimated that Olympiads spend approximately 20 crores on scholarships which is a huge deal. Sao, to make things more precise here is the list of prices or scholarships which are provided by the Olympiads to motivate students at various levels. IGKO is a single level exam that is organized for the students who are studying in classes 1 to 10. This award is received by the international, zonal and school toppers only because of their hard work and determination. Awards are generally distributed after the result declaration. In simple terms when the exam gets over then the winner will be considered eligible to get an award for successfully completing one exam. Only higher-level winners will be titled as the award receivers. For example, top 3 rank holders at international level will be recognized as winners and will receive awards. The results for top 3 international level winners are always declared after the comparison of ranks. Similarly, next rank holders will get rewards after the announcement of zonal ranks. Last but not the least next rank holder will attain class awards.

Here is the list for International level winners of Olympiad

  • ♦ 1ST prize holders will attain Rs. 50,000 as a cash prize with the gold medal and certificate to replicate your brilliance and extraordinary performance.
  • ♦Each student who will be entitled as runner up will definitely receive a cash reward of Rs.25,000 with a shining silver medal. A complimentary certificate will be also provided to the second position holder.
  • ♦ Students who acquired 3rd position in the Olympiad will receive Rs. 10,000 as a cash prize complemented with a bronze medal and certificate for your brilliant performance.

List of prizes or rewards at Zonal Level.

  • ♦ Students who will bang 1st position will receive a cash prize of Rs.5,000 each with the glittering gold medal and the certificate complementing the intelligence.
  • ♦ 2nd position holders will get Rs. 2,500 each with a shining silver medal and a certificate to showcase your achievement.
  • ♦ Second runner up will receive Rs. 1,000 as a cash prize with the addition of bronze and certificate.
  • ♦Gifts worth Rs. 1,000 each with medals and certificates will be distributed to those excellent students who get 4th till 10th position at zonal level of Olympiad.
  • ♦11th to 25th position holders will receive distinction certificates accompanied by medals of distinction. Further these bright students will attain Zonal excellence certificate.
  • ♦Last but not the least students who will achieve rank 26th + will be rewarded with an appreciation certificate.
  • ♦If one or more students acquire the same position, to appreciate their brilliance cash rewards will be distributed on the basis of below mentioned criteria.

A) International level winners

  • - The cash prize will be distributed likewise if 10 students are obtaining similar rank. To appreciate their efforts complementary certificates and medals will be provided if they fit in the applicable category.
  • -If the number of students exceeds 10 then, to appreciate them, a medal (in the relevant group) certificate and gift worth Rs. 1,000 will be provided to them.

B)Zonal level winners

  • - 5 students who will bang same position in the Olympiad will be entitled as award receivers. The cash prize will be equally distributed. Further they will get complementary medals and appreciation certificates.
  • -By chance more than 5 students attain the same position at zonal level then they will also get gift vouchers worth Rs. 1,000, certificate and medals.

C) AT SCHOOL LEVEL

  • - TThose who will secure top positions at the school level will receive gold plated precious medals with certificates of excellence. .
