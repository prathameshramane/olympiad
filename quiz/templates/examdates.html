Olympiads Fee Structure


For classes 2 to 11, the fee per subject per student is Rs. 225 for students studying and residing in India. It is $10 (USD) per subject per student for students studying and residing outside India. While for class 1, the fee per subject (English, Mathematics and Science) per student is Rs. 175 for students studying and residing in India. It is $8 (USD) per subject per student for students studying and residing outside India.

3. Your slot details will appear on the dashboard once your payment is confirmed for the subject(s). You can book your
4. Final Exam time from this page. The slot time once booked can be changed later with payment of a nominal fee.

6. In Mock Tests, the ranking will not be declared.
