Cyber

The International School Cyber Olympiad (ISCO) of the year 2020 will be conducted online. Participants can vary from classes 1-12. Students wishing to take part can give three mock tests followed by one final test. The computer olympiad helps in identifying, guiding and nurturing students interested in the fields of science, technology and the IT sector. The exam consists of multiple choice questions (MCQ type). The total time provided for the examination will be for classes 1 and for classes 2 to 12. After evaluation of results of the cyber olympiad, participants will be provided with a detailed report on their performance. This report will be divided into specific sections and categories helping students understand their strengths and weaknesses.
