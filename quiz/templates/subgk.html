General Knowledge

The International School General Knowledge Olympiad (IGKO) is an online olympiad held for students of class 1- 12. Students taking part need to appear for three mock exams followed by one final paper. The total time allotted to students for completion of the exam is minutes for classes 1 to 4 and minutes for classes 5 to 12. The pattern of the exam follows the MCQ type (Multiple Choice Questions). The GK olympiad 2020 facilitates the provision of an all-inclusive report of a student’s performance which will be useful in comprehending his/her strengths and weaknesses. For any kind of help regarding exam tips, guidance or book recommendations, please visit the exam preparation section.

The exam will be divided into sections as follows:

Class 1-12:   General Awareness
Current Affairs
Life Skills
